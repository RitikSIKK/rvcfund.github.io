About Us

The history of NITT has given birth to a great many entrepreneurs. Many more have embarked or will embark on the entrepreneurship journey, to make the world a better place. Many among them seek financial investment, mentorship, and guidance as they navigate the challenging business environments.

Rockfort Ventures is a committed attempt to develop a forum and a platform leveraging the global network of Alumni, who have deep expertise in supporting entrepreneurial ecosystems founded and promoted by NITT Alumni across many different industry sectors.

FAQs

For Entrepreneurs

  • Eligibility: Any NITT Alum who has founded or co-founded an entity, can pitch in for investment.
  • Investment Sectors: Sector agnostic.
  • Stage of Operations: Any stage in the startup progression: Ideation stage to Growth and Expansion stage.
  • Fund Request Limits: No specific limits on fund requests.

For Investors

  • Pre-Pitch: Submit the details requested in Preliminary Information for Investment form.
  • Pitch: Make a presentation to the Members once a month via Google Meet.
  • Post-Pitch: Entrepreneurs can expect feedback within 30 days.
